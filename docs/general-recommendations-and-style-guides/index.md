# General recommendations and style guides

The following pages set **a style guide** for this project, whitch **you must follow**, because consistency is important and makes work in a team much better. There will also be some general recommendations that will make your and your team's work much easier.

Most of the time you must follow the rules, but in rare cases you can break them if you think it would benefit everyone.
